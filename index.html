Frequently Asked Questions

What is a factorial?
A factorial of a non-negative integer n is the product of all positive integers less than or equal to n. It is denoted by n!.
How does the iterative method work?
The iterative method calculates the factorial using a loop, multiplying the numbers sequentially.
How does the recursive method work?
The recursive method calculates the factorial by calling itself with a decremented value until it reaches the base case (n = 0 or 1).
